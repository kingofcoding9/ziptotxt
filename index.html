NDEF-only replication

This app can save and write back NDEF payloads such as text, URLs, JSON, vCards, MIME records, and other readable NDEF bytes. It cannot copy a tag serial number, emulate your phone as a tag, or clone secure cards, access badges, transit cards, or payment cards.
